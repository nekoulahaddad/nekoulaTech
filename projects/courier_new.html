Courier Marketplace App

FindCourier

FindCourier connects people who need delivery with verified couriers. The product focuses on fast listing creation, route-based search, real-time messaging, and trustworthy profiles.

React Native + Expo Real-time Chat Deep Links Push Notifications

Product Overview

Users create delivery ads with routes, dates, weight, price, and photos. Couriers discover jobs through a route-based search flow, chat with the requester, and move deliveries through active and completed states.

Ad Marketplace

Active and completed listings with status tracking, photos, and pricing.

Messaging Layer

One-to-one conversations, notifications, and real-time updates.

Trust Signals

Profiles, reviews, verification, and favorites to build confidence.

User Flows

Clear paths for requesters and couriers

Requester Flow

  • Create an ad with route, dates, weight, and budget.
  • Attach photos and comments for package details.
  • Chat with couriers and confirm the delivery.
  • Track active deliveries and close completed jobs.

Courier Flow

  • Search deliveries by route and dates.
  • Open listing details, save favorites, and message customers.
  • Manage active and completed deliveries in one hub.
  • Collect reviews and grow profile reputation.
