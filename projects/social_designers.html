DesignersHub

DesignersHub is a social platform where designers share portfolios, publish posts, follow peers, and collaborate through messaging. It combines a community feed with professional profiles.

Django Notifications Profiles Media

Product Overview

The platform provides designer profiles, blog posts, member directories, and real-time notifications. Users can showcase work, follow creators, and build collaborative networks in one space.

Profiles & Portfolios

Designer pages with work galleries and experience details.

Community Feed

Posts, likes, and comments to grow visibility.

Messaging & Alerts

Notifications and direct messaging for collaboration.

User Flows

From profile to collaboration

Designer Flow

  • Create a profile and upload portfolio projects.
  • Publish posts to the community feed.
  • Receive likes, comments, and notifications.
  • Connect with peers via messages.

Discovery Flow

  • Browse designers by category or skill.
  • Open portfolio cards and saved posts.
  • Follow creators and build a network.
  • Contact designers for collaboration.
