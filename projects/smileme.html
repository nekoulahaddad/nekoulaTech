SmileMe

SmileMe is a mobile app with exclusive comedy videos from bloggers and comedians. AI analyzes smiles while you watch and recommends similar clips you reacted to most.

Mobile Smile AI Personalization Exclusive Content

Product Overview

The platform hosts licensed comedy clips, measures user smiles in real time, and adapts the feed based on which creators and formats trigger the best reactions.

Exclusive Videos

Curated clips from bloggers, comedians, and partner creators.

Smile Analysis

On-device vision models detect smiles and laughter intensity.

Smart Recommendations

Personalized feed based on positive emotional response signals.

User Flows

From first laugh to tailored feed

Viewer Journey

  • Open the feed and watch featured comedy clips.
  • Smile detection runs while the video is playing.
  • Like, follow, or save creators you enjoy.
  • Receive more clips similar to your reactions.

Recommendation Loop

  • AI scores positive reactions by clip and creator.
  • The feed reorders to favor high-smile formats.
  • Trend shifts update in real time.
  • Users can reset or refine the recommendation profile.
