OpenAMRI

OpenAMRI is an AI-powered news platform that pairs a conversation-driven assistant with a curated, multilingual news feed and shareable article pages.

Chat + News Multilingual Article Pages Web UI

Product Overview

The experience brings together a news listing, language switching, and a focused chat workspace for discovery. Each story can open as a dedicated page with a clean reading view.

News Feed

Structured lists of stories with clear categories and filters.

Chat Assistant

Conversational space to ask questions and summarize topics.

Shareable Pages

Readable article views with metadata and quick navigation.

User Flows

From discovery to deep reading

Reader Journey

  • Browse the latest news and select a category.
  • Open a story card to access the dedicated article view.
  • Switch language while keeping the same navigation.
  • Return to the feed with a single back action.

Chat Journey

  • Ask the assistant for a news summary or topic view.
  • Receive a structured response with key points.
  • Follow up with clarifying questions.
  • Jump back to news to verify details.
