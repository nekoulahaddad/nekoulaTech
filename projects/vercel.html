KOVKERT

KOVKERT is a multi-category marketplace for строительные материалы и товары для дома. Customers discover products through a structured catalog, search, and smart filters, then complete checkout in a few steps.

Next.js 13 PWA Redux + React Query SEO / SSR

Product Overview

The platform delivers a full e-commerce flow: category browsing, product detail pages, basket management, and a multi-step checkout. Users can track orders, manage profiles, save favorites, and chat with shops.

Catalog & Search

Category navigation, filters, and offer blocks for fast discovery.

Basket & Checkout

Item grouping by shops, discounts, and a guided order flow.

Account & Orders

Profiles, favorites, reviews, and order tracking from one hub.

User Flows

Buyer journey and marketplace flow

Buyer Flow

  • Browse categories, search, and open product pages.
  • Add items to basket and compare shop offers.
  • Checkout with delivery details and confirmation.
  • Track orders, leave reviews, and manage favorites.

Marketplace Flow

  • Shops publish products with price and stock.
  • Product cards surface in offers and search results.
  • Users can message shops directly from the site.
  • Orders route through a centralized checkout session.
