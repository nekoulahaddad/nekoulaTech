AppointControl

AppointControl helps medical teams upload clinical protocols, filter reports, and verify compliance. It provides dashboards, anomaly flags, and appointment control analytics in one workspace.

React + MUI Reports Medical Data Secure Access

Product Overview

The platform lets staff upload Excel protocols, analyze appointment compliance, and review filtered reports by doctor and ICD-10 codes. Data tables and charts provide fast insights into medical performance.

Protocol Upload

Drag-and-drop Excel files with validation and progress states.

Analytics & Charts

User Flows

From upload to medical compliance

Administrator Flow

  • Upload clinical protocol files.
  • Filter reports by doctor and ICD codes.
  • Review flagged mismatches and warnings.
  • Export results for compliance audits.

Analytics Flow

  • Open dashboards for doctor statistics.
  • Inspect tables of appointments and standards.
  • Monitor status distribution with charts.
  • Share findings with clinical leadership.
