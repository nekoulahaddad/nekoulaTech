ContentFactory

ContentFactory automates social publishing across platforms with AI rewriting, scheduling, and Telegram-based control. It standardizes content rules per channel and tracks delivery.

Product Overview

The platform connects social accounts, rewrites posts by platform rules, and publishes content with scheduling and tracking. It supports payments, subscriptions, and localized interfaces through i18n.
