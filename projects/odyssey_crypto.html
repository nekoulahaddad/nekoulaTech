NovaChain

NovaChain is a crypto trading platform with markets, real-time charts, a token launchpad, and deposit/withdraw flows. It emphasizes clarity, speed, and trust signals.

Next.js 14 Tailwind UI Charts + Data Multi-locale

Product Overview

The platform showcases live markets, analytics dashboards, and a launchpad for new token sales. Users can explore pricing, view notifications, and move funds with clear deposit and withdrawal flows.

User Flows

From discovery to trading

Trader Flow

  • Browse markets and filter assets by volume.
  • Open charts and review performance indicators.
  • Deposit funds and switch between wallets.
  • Track notifications and risk warnings.

Launchpad Flow

  • Review upcoming token sales and timelines.
  • Check allocation rules and participation tiers.
  • Join the sale from the launchpad card.
  • View confirmations and follow-up steps.
